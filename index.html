Our Story

Why We Made Bublo

We picked the name Bublo because it is short, simple, and easy to remember. We wanted a name that sounded cool and was easy to say.

We started Bublo for our financial literacy class. The goal was to learn how to run a real business, make a budget, and work together as a team. We picked boba tea because it is a really popular drink that a lot of people enjoy.

We are trying to make money while also learning how business works in real life. It has been a really fun experience so far.
